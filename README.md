* **The "Zero Posting" Effect:** Consistent posting is critical; engagement metrics plummet during content gaps, confirming the hypothesis that "out of sight is out of mind."
* **Demographic Targeting:** surprisingly, **Senior Adult** audiences delivered the highest reach and engagement, contradicting the assumption that only Gen-Z drives social traffic.
